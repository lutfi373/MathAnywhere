Journey of Differentiation

  • Introduction to Derivatives

    Start your journey by understanding what derivatives are and why they are fundamental in calculus. Learn how derivatives describe the rate of change and the basics of differentiation.

  • The Definition of Derivative

    Explore the formal definition of the derivative as a limit. Understand how the derivative represents the slope of the tangent line and the instantaneous rate of change at a point.

  • Rules of Differentiation

    Master the essential rules for finding derivatives, including the power rule, product rule, quotient rule, and chain rule. These rules make differentiation of complex functions easier.

  • Equation of Tangent

    Learn how to find the equation of the tangent line to a curve at a given point using derivatives. Tangent lines are key for approximations and understanding local behavior of functions.

  • Second Derivative

    Discover the meaning of the second derivative and how it relates to concavity and inflection points. The second derivative provides deeper insight into the shape of graphs.

  • Implicit Differentiations

    Tackle equations where y is not isolated. Implicit differentiation allows you to find derivatives even when variables are mixed together in an equation.

  • Linear Approximations & Differentials

    Use derivatives to approximate values of functions near a point. Learn about differentials and how linear approximations simplify complex calculations.
