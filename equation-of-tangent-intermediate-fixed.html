Question 1: What is a tangent line?

Answer: "A tangent line is a straight line that touches a curve at a single point."

Other acceptable words: contacts, meets, intersects

Explanation: A tangent line touches the curve at exactly one point and has the same slope as the curve at that point.

Question 2: How to find slope of tangent line

Correct Answer: A) Take the derivative of the function and evaluate it at the point

Explanation: The derivative f'(x) gives us the slope of the tangent line at any point x.

Step-by-step:

  • Final: y = 4x - 4

Question 5: Apply point-slope form

Answer: y = 5x - 2

Step-by-step:

  • Given: m = 5, point (1, 3)
  • Point-slope: y - 3 = 5(x - 1)
  • Expand: y - 3 = 5x - 5
  • Solve for y: y = 5x - 5 + 3
  • Final: y = 5x - 2
