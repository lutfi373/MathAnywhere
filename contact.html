Definition

The Derivative of \( f(x) \) with respect to \(x\) is the function \( f'(x) \) and is defined as:

\[ f'(x) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x+h) - f(x)}{h} \]

Key Concepts

Understanding the derivative involves several key concepts:

  • Limit: The fundamental idea behind the derivative.
  • Slope of a Curve: How steep a curve is at a particular point.
  • Instantaneous Rate of Change: The derivative represents this rate.
