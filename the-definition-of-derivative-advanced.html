📝 Advanced Answer Guide

Advanced-level solutions for complex derivative problems:

Question 1: f(x) = 1/√(3x-2)

Step 1: f(x+h) = 1/√(3(x+h)-2) = 1/√(3x+3h-2)

Step 2: Difference quotient: [1/√(3x+3h-2) - 1/√(3x-2)]/h

Step 3: After rationalization: f'(x) = -3/[2(3x-2)^(3/2)]

Question 2: Implicit derivative of x³ + y³ = 3xy

Answer: dy/dx = (3y - 3x²)/(3y² - 3x) = (y - x²)/(y² - x)

Question 3: Third derivative of f(x) = x⁴ - 6x³ + 12x² - 8x + 1

Answer: f'''(x) = 24x - 36

f'(x) = 4x³ - 18x² + 24x - 8

f''(x) = 12x² - 36x + 24

f'''(x) = 24x - 36

Question 4: Parametric derivative

Answer: dy/dx = (2t + 2)/(3t² - 3) = 2(t + 1)/[3(t² - 1)]

Question 5: Chain rule derivative of f(x) = sin(x² + 3x)

Answer: f'(x) = cos(x² + 3x) · (2x + 3)

Using the chain rule: d/dx[sin(u)] = cos(u) · du/dx where u = x² + 3x
